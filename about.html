About BSG

BSG Construction Corp. operates with services in new construction, renovation, environment renovation and structure addition. Created by Bruno Otávio Santos, a Christian, father and husband, whose experience of more than 15 years in the sector (since 2009), has been applied in the American market since 2023, combining his know-how of projects in Latin America with the technique and technology in the United States.

Our Founder

Bruno Otávio Santos brings over 15 years of construction expertise to every project. As a dedicated Christian, loving father, and devoted husband, Bruno founded BSG Construction Corp with a commitment to excellence and integrity that reflects his personal values.

Our Experience

Since 2009, we have been perfecting our craft in the construction industry. Our journey began in Latin America, where we developed a deep understanding of quality construction techniques and project management. In 2023, we expanded our operations to the American market, bringing our proven expertise to serve clients in the United States.

Our Approach

We combine the best of both worlds - the innovative techniques and attention to detail we've mastered in Latin America with the advanced technology and standards of the American construction industry. This unique blend allows us to deliver exceptional results that exceed our clients' expectations.
